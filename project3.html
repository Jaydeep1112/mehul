Overview

The Clever Harvey Data Analytics course gave me real-world exposure to how data informs business decisions. I worked with tools like Excel and Google Sheets to clean, analyze, and visualize data through charts, dashboards, and statistical formulas. From understanding consumer behavior to predicting trends, I learned how to extract meaningful insights from raw numbers. The hands-on approach—including case studies and mock business scenarios—taught me practical applications of logic, quantitative thinking, and storytelling with data. It sparked a strong interest in analytics and set a foundation for deeper exploration in business, technology, and economics.

  • Learn tools like Excel and Google Sheets
