Project Details

Overview

Living in a world where data is growing more and more important, I was curious to learn how numbers can tell stories, solve issues, and influence decisions. That curiosity prompted me to undertake the Clever Harvey Data Analytics Course, a real-world and industry-relevant certification that made my eyes open to what data can do.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

This course familiarized me with the core principles of business analytics and data science — such as data interpretation, visualization, and data-driven decision-making. With real-world business case studies and hands-on projects, I was given hands-on experience with applications such as Excel and Google Sheets while having a deeper understanding of statistical thinking and analytical modeling.

What made the experience worthwhile was its emphasis on applying concepts in practical scenarios — understanding consumer behavior, streamlining business processes, and interpreting market trends.

Skills That Cleared My Mind

The course honed my critical thinking, problem-solving, and quantitative skills. I discovered how to:

  • Clean and tidy raw data
  • Apply formulas and logic to conclude trends
  • Craft engaging charts and dashboards to share insights
  • Think from a technical as well as business point of view

These skills have proved to be highly useful when working on school projects and have generated more interest in areas such as economics, computer science, and business analytics.

Taking Initiative & Building Future Readiness

One of the most rewarding aspects of this course was the initiative it represented. Pursuing a professional certification outside of my school curriculum challenged me to manage my time, learn independently, and immerse myself in an unfamiliar but exciting field. It reaffirmed my belief that learning doesn’t stop at textbooks — and that stepping outside your comfort zone is where the real growth happens.

This experience has motivated me to further venture out into data analytics, potentially as a part of my academic or professional pursuit in the future, and it's provided me with a great head start on comprehending how technology and data are changing the world around us.

Objectives

  • Understand how data influences business
  • Learn tools like Excel and Google Sheets
  • Work on case-based analytics scenarios
  • Create dashboards & visualizations
  • Apply insights to real-world situations
