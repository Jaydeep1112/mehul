Project Details

Overview

I played a pivotal role in organising a large-scale Model United Nations (MUN) conference in school, which brought together over 200 students across institutions. As a core committee member and co-chair of the Business, Labor, and Sustainability (BLS) Committee, I managed operations, designed committee agendas, coordinated logistics, and led debates. The experience demanded leadership, diplomacy, and critical thinking—especially in moderating high-pressure sessions and ensuring inclusivity among first-time and experienced delegates. Beyond the event itself, it helped create a culture of open discourse and global awareness, while sharpening my skills in communication, planning, and team collaboration.

Objectives

  • Host a school-wide MUN with 200+ delegates
  • Lead as co-chair of the BLS committee
  • Coordinate schedules, debates, and logistics
  • Promote meaningful global discussion
  • Foster inclusive participation & youth voices
