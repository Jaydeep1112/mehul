Overview

Over the years, I have led the planning and execution of multiple school and community events including Environment Day, Independence Day, Traditional Day, Annual Day, and more. These experiences gave me hands-on exposure to event management—from building event flowcharts and managing backstage operations to handling crises and coordinating across departments like tech, stage, and decor. I often worked behind the scenes to ensure seamless performances, meaningful engagement, and timely communication among student teams and staff. This journey taught me how to lead calmly under pressure, think strategically, and turn ideas into memorable experiences that foster school spirit and unity.

Objectives

  • Coordinate logistics across school departments
  • Foster teamwork and collaboration
  • Ensure seamless execution of stage performances
  • Encourage community pride and participation
