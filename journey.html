“The highlights, milestones, and lessons along the way.”

1. IGCSE Excellence: A* Across the Board

I achieved A* grades in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and ICT during my Cambridge IGCSE examinations. These subjects stretched my intellectual boundaries and fueled my passion for logic, structure, and discovery. Scoring high wasn’t the goal — understanding deeply was. These results are a reflection of my curiosity, discipline, and genuine love for learning.

My foundation in these subjects has been vital for my academic confidence and has guided me in tackling challenges in engineering, computer science, and data analysis — both in and outside the classroom.

2.Trinity Certified Speaker: Communication as a Core Skill

Completing all 8 grades of the Trinity College London Communication Skills certification was a transformative journey. From spontaneous speeches and persuasive presentations to thoughtful dialogue and group communication, I learned not just to speak — but to connect.

This program shaped my emotional intelligence, sharpened my presence in public forums, and gave me the confidence to represent, lead, and inspire — skills I carry into MUNs, debates, and team collaborations today.

3.Clever Harvey – Data Analytics Certification

Through the Clever Harvey Data Analytics course, I learned to transform raw data into meaningful decisions. From cleaning datasets and applying formulas to crafting dashboards and visualizations, I developed a strong command of tools like Excel and Google Sheets.

What stood out most was the real-world application — analyzing market trends, understanding consumer behavior, and applying statistical logic to business cases. This course helped me bridge the gap between theory and practice, and sparked an interest in data science and business analytics.

4.Faida’s E-Commerce Platform

As part of a business-technology partnership between Span Systems and Faida (based in Nairobi), I had the opportunity to contribute to a real-world e-commerce solution. My role involved working on the platform’s design and logic — from UI/UX flows and vendor onboarding to payment integration and backend data structure.

More than the technical side, what inspired me was Faida’s mission: to empower small local vendors and bring underserved communities into the digital economy. This experience helped me understand how technology, when built with purpose, can uplift people far beyond code.

5.Peer Tutoring: Leading by Teaching

What began as a favor to friends turned into a structured and fulfilling responsibility. As a peer tutor, I helped fellow students understand complex topics in science, coding, and mathematics — simplifying tough ideas, prepping them for exams, and providing a safe space to ask questions.

Through this, I developed patience, clarity in communication, and a sense of shared success. Watching others grow through my guidance was not only rewarding but also deepened my own understanding of the subjects I loved.
