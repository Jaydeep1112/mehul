Project Details

Times School – Featured Coverage

Rustomjee Cambridge International School & Junior College continues to shape future leaders through real-world exposure, hands-on learning, and compassionate initiatives. The school encourages students to go beyond classrooms and bring positive change.

Teaching Humanity in Nairobi’s Kibera Slums

Rustomjee Cambridge A-Level students visited Nairobi’s Kibera Slums, one of the world’s largest slums. They worked with the Red Rhino Outreach Center, teaching educational activities, hygiene skills, and social responsibility to children. The students returned with a new sense of gratitude, responsibility, and purpose.

Igniting Young Minds with AI

Through the “Deep Dive AI Camp,” students of grades 7–8 learned AI fundamentals, real-world applications, and risk-taking through innovation. The camp boosted creativity, confidence, and leadership, helping students develop presentations and prototypes that sparked meaningful conversations on AI’s future.

Mentoring the Future Through AI

Students from Class of 2026 organized an AI Project Camp where they taught juniors how to code, build AI models, and analyze real-world problems. This peer-led initiative helped younger learners understand AI while allowing senior students to grow as leaders and mentors.

Mastering German, Mentoring Juniors

Mithi Shinde (Class of 2027) excelled in German language studies and mentored juniors through the school’s self-study program. She developed a passion for teaching and cultural exchange, organizing speaking sessions and interactive lessons for younger students.

Silencing Bullies, Empowering Voices

Class of 2026 A-Level students launched an anti-bullying campaign to create a safer, more inclusive school. Through presentations, posters, videos, and theatre, they educated juniors about empathy and helped them stand up against unhealthy behavior.

A Legacy of Strategic Vision

Rustomjee Cambridge International Schools aim to nurture leaders through academic excellence, innovation, and compassion. With campuses in Dahisar, Virar, and Thane, the school continues to rank among India’s best for Cambridge education.
