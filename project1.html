Project Details

Overview

As part of the Clever Harvey Junior MBA in Entrepreneurship, I developed Clauset—a curated clothing brand focused on sustainable fashion through the sale of pre-loved and designer thrift pieces. I designed the brand’s identity, sourced initial inventory, built a basic online presence, and explored strategies for consumer engagement. More than just a class project, Clauset was my first venture into startup thinking—identifying market gaps, understanding customer personas, creating value propositions, and experimenting with branding, pricing, and marketing channels. This experience not only sharpened my business instincts but also made me appreciate the power of ideas that serve both purpose and people.

Objectives

  • Create a youth-led sustainable fashion brand
  • Apply real-world business concepts
  • Learn branding, customer personas & value propositions
  • Build confidence in taking action on ideas
  • Explore fashion's role in conscious consumerism
