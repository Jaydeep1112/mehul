About Me

I am a passionate and purpose-driven student who thrives at the intersection of innovation, empathy, and leadership. My journey has been shaped by a commitment to personal growth through academics, communication, technology, and social contribution.

My foundation in communication, honed through all eight levels of the Trinity College London program, has made me a confident speaker, empathetic listener, and collaborative leader. It’s not just about what I say, but how I connect — whether in debates, presentations, or everyday teamwork.

Outside academics, football has been a constant anchor, teaching me tenacity, discipline, and team spirit. Playing since grade 4, the field has taught me lessons in leadership and resilience — ones that extend far beyond the scoreboard into how I face challenges and uplift those around me.
