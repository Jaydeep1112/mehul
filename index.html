About Nyati Group

Nyati Group stands among India’s most renowned real estate brands. With over 28 years of engineering excellence, the Group has been at the forefront of creating exquisite realty spaces ranging from residential projects, IT parks, commercial complexes, hospitals, hotels, and institutional buildings.

Over the years, Nyati Group has successfully delivered more than 70 Real Estate projects, developed over 40 million sq. ft., shaping urban landscapes and fulfilling aspirations. Today, the Group takes pride in having created homes for 15,000+ happy families, building not just structures but complete ecosystems that foster community living. This growing family is a true reflection of the trust and goodwill that Nyati Group continues to inspire.

Driven by a commitment to impeccable quality, timely delivery, and technological innovation, Nyati Group has emerged as one of Pune’s most-preferred real estate brands. Every project stands as a benchmark in design, execution, and customer-centricity, setting higher standards for the industry.

Our vision extends beyond construction—we believe in building enduring relationships with our customers, stakeholders, and partners. Over the years, this approach has been recognized with prestigious accolades including the Sakal Maha-Brand Award 2023 in the Real Estate category, Pune’s Preferred Real Estate Brand 2024 by Times Power Brands Pune 2024, and Brand of the Year 2024 by Marksmen Daily, among many others.

Each milestone reaffirms Nyati Group’s legacy of trust, excellence, and innovation, as we continue our pursuit to be the first choice - today, tomorrow, and forever.
